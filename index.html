My Favorite Nintendo Games

By Christy Cheng


Introduction
 

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

One of my favorite Nintendo Switch games is Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. In this 2017-released title, our protagonist saves the world and his love by slaying monsters, collecting equipment, and solving puzzles. This open-world game is sure to keep you preoccupied with its breath-taking soundtrack, countless hours of gameplay, and DLC options. I would reccomend this game to anybody. It is truly an experience for anyone to enjoy.

 

Super Smash Brothers Ultimate

When playing with friends, my go-to Nintendo Switch game would be Super Smash Brothers Ultimate. Not only can you play this action-packed thrill ride with up to 8 people at the same time, you can even fight your friends with a character list comprised of seventy-four fighters. This game is sure to keep anybody entertained for quite some time. When playing Super Smash Brothers, I love playing Kirby.

 

Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Maybe endless adventure or a free-for-all brawl isn't something you would be interested in. If these titles aren't your cup of tea, then why not try a relaxing town simulator where you can build a neighborhood from scratch, make friends, and watch your community grow! It is my favorite game to play after a long stressful day of class. This game even allows you to connect with your friends and visit each other's islands, where you can even buy and sell produce.
